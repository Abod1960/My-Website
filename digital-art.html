Me and Digital Art
Throughout history, art was very spotlighted and appreciated by the public, the rulers, and even the world, actually, I don't think that anyone on this earth is opposing art at least he should like one of the art fields, and one of these people is me, I was impressed by the beauty of art and how it can send messages and change opinions and ideas. And from this understanding I started learning how to create artworks and how to paint and play with brushes and canvas, on the other hand, I found a new pretty interesting art style, which is the Digital Art, and that was a game-changing move for me especially when I saw some really traditional art styles done with Photoshop or Procreate,* so I decided to move to digital art sector and I actually started painting some artworks and through my journey, I was uploading my artworks to Artstation and you can check my portfolio here.
